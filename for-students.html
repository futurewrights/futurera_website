For students

Yes, this works for you too.

Most "AI for high schoolers" content is either patronizing or sells you a video course where someone reads bullet points off a slide. Futurera is trying to do something different. We treat you like the working professional you're three years from being.

If you're a Grade 9–12 student wondering whether this is for you — read on. We tried to write this without the parent-voice most education sites get stuck in.

What you'll actually do

→

Build, not just talk about

Real AI projects you can show — chatbots, research tools, automations. Your work goes on the public internet. Your friends can open it on their phones.

→

Get real mentorship

Working AI engineers and senior university faculty — not generic tutors reading off slides. Weekly 1:1s. Direct feedback on real work.

→

Get paid (eventually)

Top performers get invited into AI Studio. Paid AI work at age 16+. Real clients, real stipend.

Three paths, depending on what you want

Everyone starts the same way: in AI Lab — our foundation program. From there, you pick what fits your goals.

Start here

AI Lab

Build small AI projects end-to-end. Learn to think with AI, not just type prompts at it. Multiple courses, multiple formats — find what fits your schedule.

Academic path

University Track

Aiming at competitive CS, Data, or Health Tech programs? Leave with a real portfolio and a research artifact you can talk about in an admissions interview.

Industry path

Industry Track

More interested in working — internships, real teams, shipping? We prep you for the interviews and day-one realities of a junior role.

The apex — paid

AI Studio — where we pay students to ship real work

Top performers from University Track or Industry Track get invited into AI Studio. Real client engagements from external companies. You get paid (stipend, age 16+, WSIB-covered), you ship under a senior practitioner, and you mentor younger AI Lab students. Learn · Coach · Deliver.

What we won't do

  • Tell you AI is magic. It has real limits and real failure modes — you'll learn both.
  • Hand you a chatbot and call it learning. You'll build, write, and explain. The chatbot is a tool, not the lesson.
  • Promise you'll get into your dream school. We can't. We can give you the work that makes the application a real conversation.
  • Filter by whether you're a "coding kid." We care if you'll show up, ship something, and be honest about what you don't know yet.
Bring the energy of the working professional you're three years from being — we'll match it.
