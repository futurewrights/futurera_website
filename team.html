About our team

Faculty. Practitioners. Mentors.

Future Wrights is built by a multidisciplinary team of educators, engineers, researchers, and founders working across academia and industry.

University-affiliated faculty help shape the learning experience and academic direction, while AI practitioners bring real-world perspective through modern tools, workflows, and project-based mentorship.

Each cohort is additionally supported by graduate mentors and researchers who guide students through hands-on learning, creative exploration, and collaborative problem solving.
